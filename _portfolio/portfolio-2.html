--- title: "Adversarial Training Framework for automatic testing of autonomous systems" excerpt: "" collection: portfolio --- This project is done in collaboration with Cyber-Physical Systems Verification, Intelligence, Design and Analysis [(CPS-VIDA)](https://cps-vida.github.io/) lab at USC. In this project, we aim to come up with a framework for quantifying and improving robustness of agents trained in a specific way against adversarial agents.
